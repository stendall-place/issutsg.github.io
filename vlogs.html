Video Logs!

So this year, ISS decided to go techno-savvy and start a series of video blogs that would effectively promote our events! It also gave our members a chance to get to know our team on a more informal basis. Containing handy tips and tricks on how to survive student life in Toronto, the vlogs proved to be very helpful to our audience! These vlogs are not restricted to our Executive Members so if you would like a cameo, shoot us an email so we can get ready for your close up! :)

Our 2nd vlog! And we outdid ourselves

As a follow up to our first ever vlog venture, we filmed our second one for the month of October! Full of details about our events, this vlog basically covered the workings of ISS that month! These include the smashing success that was the Social Mixer, BC Party, and the Inter-mural Soccer Tournament that we took part in. We also included a short snippet of all the super fun places on campus that we think are top notch, such as, the Rotman Cafe, and the UC Quad! We are yet to release our third vlog so stay tuned! We have oodles of fun lined up for you all! :)

First ever vlog... At UTM!

This was our very own version of a maiden voyage! Niharika Tugnait and Prashanti Laxmi, both members of our Executive Team covered the Annual Cricket Tournament at the University of Toronto, Mississauga. ISS Blacks, our beloved team from St. George, were in the finals against the UTM team. Sadly, we lost the Tournament but this was an uber fun day for all of us! This vlog also contained an interview of one of our players (who happens to be an Executive Member) and a lowdown of the upcoming events that ISS was planning. If any of you want to be a part of this tournament next year just give us a shoutout or join our Facebook group named 'ISS Sports'
