Culture Night 2013

This is your opportunity to experience India to its fullest as ISS brings you art, food and sport desi ishtyle. Culture Night 2013 with its theme ‘Kashmir to Kanyakumari’ was a magical train journey covering the north, south, east and west of India as we brought you food and activities from different parts of the country. Whether it was Kashmiri chai or Bollywood karaoke, from Jaipur’s block painting to Kolkata’s kathi rolls, we really didn’t miss a beat. To ice the cake, we had the ISS Blues Dance team rock the stage with classical, garba, bhangra AND Bollywood (check out our awesome performance below!)

Keep your eyes out for Culture Night 2014 to see where we take you next.
