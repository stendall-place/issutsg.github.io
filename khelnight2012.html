Khel Night 2012

Following the Jeopardy! format, ISS hosted a game show covering topics ranging from India’s politics and culture to the sport and movie scene. Everybody put on their thinking caps, as the teams enthusiastically fought to press the buzzer first and be the Khel Night champions! And why not? Winners got complimentary tickets to our next event, Culture Night!

First of its kind amongst the ISS events, the night was a great hit and everybody enjoyed the competition…and of course the yummy samosas that were served.
