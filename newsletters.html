Newsletters!

Our newsletters are your one stop shop to get to know everything ISS as well as everything desi within the city! This is our way of connecting with you and providing you with the latest ISS updates and experiences of our varied student body. Our newsletters come out twice every semester to keep you in the loop of what’s Indian on campus. We are always looking for volunteers to write for our newsletter, so please do give us a shout if you are interested! Now scroll down and check them out, we hope you enjoy them!

The one where it all started.

August 2013 ‘The First Years’ issue: Our very first issue of the ISS newsletter especially for you first years! From some handy U of T tips to some desi tips to prevent you from feeling homesick, this newsletter has it all to make your experience here an extremely pleasant one!

Check it out »

Back with a bang!

September 2013 ‘The Frosh Special’ issue: Our second newsletter acts as a guide to the best of Toronto. From a ‘TIFF special’ to a list of Toronto’s hottest spots, this newsletter acts as a resource of information into ways of enjoying this city. We also have one of our execs write about her lovely experience working in India and some insight on ISS’s associated charity organization PaperKite Children’s Foundation AND last but not the least a quick and easy recipe, tried and tested by our in house Gordon Ramsey! ;)

Check it out »

Third time’s a charm

January 2014 ‘Winter is here’ issue: Bringing the new year with a new newsletter, this issue gives you tested and tried tips to face the winter and enjoy your reading week! To commemorate Republic Day, check out our picture contest and win a VIP package to our most awaited event, DIL SE! Apart from our traditional recipe section, we also have a movie review, as well as some stories and experiences of our very own U of T students! Open the link to enjoy it in its full glory!
