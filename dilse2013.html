Dil Se 2013

This is ISS’s biggest event and is eagerly awaited all year round! Dil Se is a GTA wide talent show with performances ranging from dancing to singing to comedy sketches. Every year we get a main headline act to perform as well as performers from the student community to showcase their talents. ‘Dil Se’ with its amazing performances and its mouth-watering four course meal is an evening you do not want to miss out on.

Dil Se 2013 was a huge success as the famous Indo – Pak duo JOSH rocked the stage with their best singles like Kabhi and Main Hoon Tanha on our very own St. George campus! We also had The Folksters bust some bhangra music while ISS’s dance team moved to the grooves of Shammi Kapoor and Kareena Kapoor. This amazing night was followed by a delicious meal of mattar paneer, pulao and gulab jamun.

If this is enticing enough, do watch out for what Dil Se 2014 has to offer you because we are going bigger and better.
