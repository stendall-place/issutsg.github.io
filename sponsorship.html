What's the reach?

Currently, we have more than 10K members in our steadily growing Facebook group, and a social reach of over 300 members for our prime events like Dil Se! The ISS newsletters and video blogs have a substansial viewership of over 1000 people. ISS is still in its youth, and with each year we see a pleasing growth of the group's reach.
