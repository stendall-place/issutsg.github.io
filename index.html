Welcome! Stendall Place

Stendall Place is a planned unit development located in a residential neighborhood in north Seattle. Built in the early 80’s, it is a quiet and beautifully landscaped community with mature conifer and deciduous trees and many rhododendrons. There are 18 separate buildings that have from 2 to 6 townhouse-style units in each building. Stendall Place has 67 individual units. Stendall Place is governed by homeowner association by-laws and cooperative agreements. These policies are implemented through a board made up of homeowners. The home owners’ association board employs a management company to directly provide maintenance or to hire service companies.

Stendall Place occupants strive to maintain a tranquil and respectful environment as well as a welcoming community. All residents are encouraged step out and get to know their neighbors. To help facilitate the building of community, the homeowners’ association sponsors a summer garden party and encourages homeowners to attend the monthly board meetings and to volunteer in the periodic garden work parties. Along with these planned opportunities to socialize residents are highly encouraged to be considerate when engaging in activities where noise, lighting or pet care may interfere with neighborhood peace and quiet.
