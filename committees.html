Standing Committees

The standing committees meet regularly to discuss various issues pertaining to Stendall Place and report to the board of directors. They consist of homeowners as members. The various broad caterogization of committees are as follows.

Communications Committee

The Stendall Place Communications Committee is charged with facilitating communications between the homeowners, the board, and the management company. The committee publishes a newsletter, updates the residentâ€™s directory and oversees the Stendall Place website. Along with these tasks, the committee also is responsible for welcoming new residents and publicizing meetings and events at the direction of the board.

Maintenance Committee

The Stendall Place Maintenance Committee oversees building and walkway repairs. The committee is responsible for keeping a list of repair projects and assisting the management company in defining the scope of those jobs. The committee makes recommendations to the board so that repair/replacement project spending priorities can be set.

Landscaping Committee

The Stendall Place Landscaping Committee is responsible for preserving the health and beauty of the common area plants and trees within Stendall Place. By providing guidance to the landscaping company and arborists and by organizing work parties to clear, weed and/or prune, the committee maintains and improves our common area. The committee also makes suggestions to homeowners about their foundation and walkway planting beds. The committee makes recommendations to the board so that landscaping spending priorities can be set.

Resident Concerns/Management Liaison
